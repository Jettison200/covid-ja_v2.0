About Covid-Ja

This website was developed by the Web Developers Jason Clarke and Suniel England at the begining of this crisis. This is version 3.0 with a new interactive coronavirus tracker, a revised color scheme and new animations.

The website originally started out as a small side project but quickly grew to facilitate the growing need for accurate information in Jamaica.

So far this website has been accessed by people in Jamaica, Pakistan, Germany, Isreal, USA, Greenland, Trinidad, Barbados, Antigua, Turkey, UK, Montserrat, Poland, Sweden, India etc. Were they Jamaicans? We have no idea.
